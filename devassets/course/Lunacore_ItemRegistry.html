Item Registry

Within the LunacoreAPI, there is a function to add custom items into Minecraft: New Nintendo 3ds Edition. With this method, you can have an item with a custom texture, name, recipe (Link), and category. This does not mean that items categorized as a "tool" would act as a tool, despite it being in said category. An example of this is from the example "testmod" which adds the "Copper Pickaxe," which, despite having "Pickaxe" in it's name, does not act as one.
